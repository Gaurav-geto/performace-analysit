Where We Work

Industries Served

Our consulting experience spans the most demanding sectors in engineering — each with its own operational rhythms, regulatory demands, and performance expectations.

  • Mission Critical Projects

    Zero-downtime engineering for facilities where failure is not an option — including hospitals, defence installations, and critical network nodes.

  • Power Plants

    Thermal efficiency consulting for coal, gas, and combined-cycle power stations — from condenser optimisation to balance-of-plant improvements.
